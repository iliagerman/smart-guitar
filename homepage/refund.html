Refund Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. Overview

We want you to be satisfied with Smart Guitar. This Refund Policy explains when and how you can request a refund for your subscription.

2. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account profile page. When you cancel:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
  • You will not be charged for the next billing cycle
  • You retain access to all paid features until the end of the current period

3. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

  • Within 14 days of initial purchase: If you are not satisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your first subscription payment
  • Service issues: If the Service was significantly unavailable or non-functional during your billing period, you may request a prorated refund
  • Duplicate charges: If you were charged in error or billed twice, we will issue a full refund for the duplicate charge

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are generally not provided for:

  • Requests made more than 14 days after the initial purchase (unless covered by Section 3)
  • Partial months of service after cancellation
  • Accounts suspended or terminated for violation of our Terms of Service

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at support@smart-guitar.com with:

  • Your account email address
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Your transaction or subscription ID (if available)

6. Processing

Refunds are processed through Paddle, our payment provider. Once approved, refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days depending on your payment method and financial institution.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
